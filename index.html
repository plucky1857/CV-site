About Me

I'm a learning game developer that likes creating games, just as much as playing them. My passion for creating games stems from a passion i've had for playing games since i was 6 years old. Making game mechanics and creating worlds that i've wanted to play for years is how i make my games come to life.

My personal interests lie in system design, gameplay programming, and creating player experiences that I myself and everyone else would enjoy. I'm familiar with multiple game engines and various programming languages.
